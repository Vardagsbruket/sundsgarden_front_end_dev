Breakfast bread

Ingredients

  • Wholegrain wheat flour, 4 dl
  • Water, 5 dl
  • Yeast, 12 g

  • Seeds or nuts, 2 dl
  • Wheat flour (50% wholegrain), 6-7 dl
  • Salt, 1 table spoon
  • Green or red lentils, 1 tetra pack

Instructions

Day 1
  1. Mix yeast and water until completely disolved.
  2. Add 4 dl wholegrain wheat flour and mix. Cover and let rest in room temperature for 12-24 hours.
Day 2
  1. Add salt and nuts/seeds and mix.
  2. Add 3 dl of wheat flour, little by little and mix.
  3. Rinse lentils and then mix in with the rest.
  4. Add remaining flour little by little and knead the dough. It should be easy to knead but still remain a little bit sticky.
  5. Put in an oven dish on a baking paper and cover with a clean kitchen towel.
  6. Let rest for 2 hours.

  7. Heat oven to 250 C and bake in the middle of the oven for 15 min.
  8. Lower the temperature to 225 C and bake for another 35 min.
  9. Take the bread out of the oven dish and baking paper and let cool on a grid.

Enjoy your bread and breakfast!
